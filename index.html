Welcome to Bistro 1491

Embark on a culinary journey through time, where tradition meets taste. At Bistro 1491, we take pride in crafting dishes made exclusively from ingredients found on each continent before the exchange of flavors that reshaped the world. Explore the vibrant essence of the Americas with our "Taste of the Americas" menu, featuring delicacies like Cooked Imperial Incan Avian and Hearts of the Empire, made with ancient ingredients that once graced royal feasts.

Venture across the seas with our "Taste of Afro-Eurasia" selection, highlighting rich, time-honored flavors. Savor dishes like Mansa Musa Muscle, inspired by the wealth and splendor of historic kingdoms, and Blumen Onion, crafted with legendary spices from distant lands. Every bite at Bistro 1491 is a tribute to the world as it was before Columbus set sail; join us and rediscover the flavors that shaped civilizations - one plate at a time.
